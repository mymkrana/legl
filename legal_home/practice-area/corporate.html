CORPORATE


  • Overview
  • Team
  • Publications

JSAs corporate practice is centred around transactional and legal advisory services concerning day-to-day business, regulatory issues, corporate and governance affairs. We advise on legal issues concerning inbound and outbound investments, strategic alliances, collaborations and corporate restructurings. From restructuring, mergers and acquisitions (including those in the public space) to private equity and joint ventures, we advise clients through all stages of complex and marquee assignments. We assist our clients in dealing with diverse corporate governance and compliance issues including FCPA /Anti-Bribery/Anti-Corruption investigations.

Our corporate practice is organised around 13 service lines. Our clients include multinational corporations, large Indian businesses (in private, public and joint sector). We work closely with in-house counsel, investment banks, consulting and accounting firms. We work with multilateral agencies and policy making institutions on development of policy and legal frameworks. We assist and counsel start-ups and venture backed companies by drawing upon our deep understanding of how technology companies are incorporated, financed, and grown.

Understanding the business of the client, the industry and applying years of our combined expertise allows us to provide innovative and constructive solutions in complex transactional engagements. JSAs emphasis on teamwork across its wide network of offices across India, allows us to benefit from the various specialisations available for the ultimate benefit of our clients.

Across our 8 offices, we have corporate lawyers trained in a number of disciplines, and a representative statement on our core strengths and experience is set out below. Where appropriate, we call upon our Finance and Disputes Practices to provide their capabilities to help our clients avoid exposure and reach their desired business goal. With a host of multifaceted clients, we are proud to be the preferred law firm for our chosen areas of practice.

Our Corporate Practice of JSA combines the capabilities of more than 200 lawyers across sectors, industries and practice areas.
